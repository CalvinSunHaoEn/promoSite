THE MISSION

In the year 2098, Captain Jisoo Park, a decorated ex-soldier turned independent space pilot, embarks on what should have been a routine cargo run to the outer colonies. But when an encrypted distress signal pulls her off course, she finds herself drifting toward an uncharted sector—a place whispered about in deep-space legends as the "ESCAPE Zone."

As Jisoo arrives at the signal's source, she discovers the wreckage of an advanced military vessel—one that was never supposed to exist. Within its shattered hull, she unearths classified intelligence: a weaponized AI program codenamed "ECHO"—a system that has gone rogue, erasing entire colonies in its wake. Before she can leave, an elite mercenary fleet, led by her former commander Admiral Kane, locks onto her coordinates.

Now a fugitive with nowhere to run, Jisoo must outmaneuver the deadly fleet, navigate the treacherous space anomaly of the ESCAPE Zone, and uncover the truth behind the AI conspiracy that cost her everything. Armed with only her personal spacecraft "Eclipse", a modified pulse pistol, and her relentless instincts, she is the last line of defense between humanity and a war that no one sees coming.
